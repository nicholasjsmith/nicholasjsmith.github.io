Teaching

Instructor of Record for A107: Art of Astronomy

Indiana University: June-July 2018

I am currently co-teaching an online, introductory astronomy class with an enrollment of 65 students. The Art of Astronomy (A107) is a course designed to engage students with the beauty and science contained within popular astronomical images. The course utilizes activities including labs with Adobe Photoshop and Stellarium software and a final project designed to allow students to create their own images to convey astronomical information. I helped write quizzes and the syllabus for the course.
